Platform Capabilities

Built for XRPL-native finance

Every feature is designed around RLUSD's unique position as the only regulated USD stablecoin natively issued on the XRP Ledger.

01

NYDFS-Approved Issuance

RLUSD is issued under a trust charter from the New York Department of Financial Services — the strictest stablecoin regulatory framework in the US — making it uniquely suitable for institutional use.

02

Native XRPL DEX Integration

Unlike bridged stablecoins, RLUSD uses XRPL's built-in DEX and AMM protocol — meaning swaps settle with ledger finality in under 4 seconds with fees as low as $0.001 per transaction.

03

Monthly Reserve Attestations

Every RLUSD is backed 1:1 by US dollar deposits and short-duration US Treasury bills held in segregated accounts. Independent Big-4 auditors publish monthly attestation reports publicly.

04

OFAC Sanctions Screening

RLUSD incorporates real-time OFAC SDN screening at the protocol level. Addresses flagged by Ripple's compliance engine are automatically blocked from interacting with RLUSD trust lines.

05

Cross-Chain Bridge Protocol

Ripple's official XRPL-EVM bridge lets you move RLUSD between the XRP Ledger and Ethereum in under 30 seconds without wrapping or custodial risk, enabling seamless multi-chain DeFi.

06

Ripple Payments Integration

RLUSD is natively supported in Ripple Payments — the global cross-border payment network used by 80+ banks and payment providers — enabling instant, low-cost international USD settlement.

Getting Started

How to use RLUSD in 3 steps

From wallet connection to earning yield — everything is designed to be simple, fast and secure on the XRP Ledger.

1

Connect your XRPL wallet

Link XUMM, Crossmark, or MetaMask (for Ethereum RLUSD). No seed phrase sharing — we use wallet-connect standards so you always remain in control of your keys.

2

Acquire or bridge RLUSD

Swap XRP for RLUSD directly on the XRPL DEX, bridge from Ethereum, or receive RLUSD from any counterparty. The XRP Ledger's native trust line system enables instant peer-to-peer transfers.

3

Earn, borrow or transact

Put your RLUSD to work: provide liquidity for 8%+ APR, stake for guaranteed yield, borrow against XRP collateral, or use RLUSD in Ripple Payments for global cross-border transfers.
